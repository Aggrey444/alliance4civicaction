About Us

Vision Statement

To build an informed and politically active citizenry that upholds truth, accountability, and good governance for national development and social justice."

Misssion Statement

Alliance For Civic Action (ACA) is committed to fostering civic engagement, political accountability, and democratic participation in Ghana.

Our work includes:

  • Public education and awareness campaigns.
  • Policy advocacy and civic activism.
  • Engaging grassroots communities for political participation
  • Providing training and mentorship programs.

Our Impact
    0 Citizens Engaged on Civic education
    • Active in All 16 Regions of Ghana, fostering political awareness and accountability.
    • Trained Hundreds of Young Leaders to participate in governance and decision-making.
    • Exposed Political Misinformation and promoted truth in public discourse
